About Me

I have always been interested in how things work, so I enrolled in a Software Engineering bootcamp. I really enjoy the team work and learning how applications are designed and created. Developing full stack applications is rewarding. I never there are so many resources to learn and develop software engineering skills. The MERN stack is a fun challenge and I can’t wait to get more exposure with it. I’ve just started learning React.js and Socket.io, it was so exciting to make my first real-time chat app.

In my free time, I enjoy hiking with beautiful wife and our 2 dogs. Onion Creek and McKinney Falls are our favorites. I also enjoy tinkering with my motorcycle and jeep. I added an android tablet as a radio and its awesome. My father and I did a motorcycle trip and rode `The Tail of the Dragon`, that was an amazing experience, 318 curves in 11 miles.
